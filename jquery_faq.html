10 Facts about Cactus

Long Life Cycle
Cacti life cycle lasts from 15-300 years depending on the species.

Unique Plant
Stores huge amount of water to allow it to survive in extreme temperatures

Roots
Roots can reach up to 7 feet in diameter. Cacti are focused on low depth of the roots to gather as much water as possible

Climate/Region
Almost all cacti are native to hot and dry habitats of South and North America

Species
There are almost 2000 different species of cacti

Different sizes
One of the largest species can get up to 66 feet high and weigh up to 4800 pounds when loaded with water. Smaller species are only a few inches high

Colors
Usually green, brown green or blueish. other variety of colors include red, pink, orange depending on the species and type of pollination.

Spines instead of leaves
Cacti have spines instead of leaves. The spines can be soft/rigid, curved/straight, scattered/arranged. They can grow up to 6 inches long

Spines EVERYWHERE
Spines fulfill two major roles: Prevent water loss via transpiration and to protect the plant from animals.

Help in the desert
Bees, buterflies, moths, bath and hummingbirds are main pollinators of cacti
